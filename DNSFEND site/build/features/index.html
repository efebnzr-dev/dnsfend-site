DNSFEND Features & Capabilities

Comprehensive DNS security solutions tailored for your organization.

Threat Detection & Protection

Real-Time Threat Detection

DNSFEND continuously monitors DNS requests and detects threats in real time. Requests containing potential risks are instantly blocked, protecting IT networks from malware, data breaches, and phishing attempts.

Automatic Response

Automatically block access to malicious domains or redirect users to safe websites. Malicious content is neutralized before it can reach users.

Secure Internet Experience

DNSFEND prioritizes security in employees' internet usage. By blocking access to malicious websites, it ensures a safe browsing experience and enables reliable access to IT resources.

Management & Configuration

Centralized Management

Manage all DNS security settings from a single control panel. Update settings with ease and maintain complete control over your security policies.

Customized Solutions

DNSFEND is tailored to meet the specific needs of your organization. Configure systems according to users' internet usage habits and unique security requirements.

Visibility & Reporting

Comprehensive Reporting

Get detailed reports on internet traffic, threat detection, and security status analysis. All user access and DNS queries are recorded in detail.

Complete Audit Trail

Detailed insights to analyze the security status of users and servers. Track all activities for thorough security monitoring and analysis.

Compliance & Security Standards

Compliance Ready

Full compliance with legal regulations including KVKK (Personal Data Protection Law) and other applicable legal requirements.

Legal Obligation Fulfillment

DNSFEND helps organizations fulfill their legal obligations by ensuring that access logs are properly maintained and auditable.
